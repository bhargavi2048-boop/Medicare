FAQ

Frequently asked
questions

How do I book an appointment online? +
Click "Book Appointment" in the navigation, choose your doctor, pick a date and time slot, fill in your details, and confirm. The entire process takes under 2 minutes and you'll receive an instant confirmation with your appointment ID.
Do you accept insurance? +
Yes, we accept all major health insurance providers including Star Health, HDFC Ergo, Bajaj Allianz, New India Assurance, United India, and government schemes like PMJAY (Ayushman Bharat). Cashless claim settlement is available.
Is video consultation available? +
Yes! Video consultations are available for most specialities. When booking, simply select "Video Consultation" as your visit type. You'll receive a secure link before your appointment time. Video consultations are available 7 days a week.
What should I bring for my appointment? +
Please bring a valid photo ID (Aadhaar/PAN card), your appointment ID, any previous medical reports or prescriptions, a list of current medications, and your insurance card if applicable. Arrive 15 minutes before your slot.
How do I cancel or reschedule? +
Call our helpline at 1800-123-456 (toll free) or email care@medicare.in with your appointment ID. Cancellations made at least 4 hours before the appointment time are eligible for a full refund. Rescheduling is free of charge.
What is your emergency response time? +
Our emergency department operates 24×7 with senior doctors always on duty. For life-threatening emergencies, dial 108 for an ambulance. Our internal emergency line (+91 44 2800 1111) is answered within 30 seconds, around the clock.
