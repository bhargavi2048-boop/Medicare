Compassionate care for every patient

We believe that quality healthcare is a fundamental right. Since 1998, MediCare has served over 50,000 patients across Tamil Nadu, delivering advanced medical care with a human touch.

Our Journey

25 years of
healing excellence

1998

Founded in Chennai

MediCare opens its first clinic in Teynampet with 5 doctors and a vision to change healthcare in Tamil Nadu.

2005

NABH Accreditation

MediCare becomes one of the first hospitals in Tamil Nadu to receive the prestigious NABH accreditation.

2010

Madurai Expansion

Second hospital opens in Madurai, bringing specialist care to central Tamil Nadu with 180 beds.

2017

Digital Transformation

Launch of the online booking platform — 10,000 appointments in the first year alone.

2021

Coimbatore Campus

Third hospital opens in Coimbatore with a state-of-the-art cardiac catheterisation lab.

2024

50,000 Patients Milestone

MediCare celebrates serving 50,000+ patients, with a 4.9-star average rating across all departments.
